Cancellation and Modification Policy

You may change your prepaid hotel reservation, but you will be charged the cancellation or change fee if changes requests less than 30 days prior to check-in. If you do not change your reservation before the cancellation policy period applicable to the hotel you reserved, which varies by hotel (usually 15 to 30 Days) prior to your date of arrival, you will be subject to a charge equal to applicable nightly rates, tax recovery charges and service fees. in the event you do not show for the first night of the reservation and plan to check-in for subsequent nights in your reservations changes with us no later than 30 days prior to the first night of the reservation to prevent cancellation of your reservation. If the entire reservation is cancelled you will be charged a cancellation fee equal 20% of your total Contract price. You agree to pay any cancellation or change fees that you incur in limited cases, some hotels do not permit changes to or cancellations of reservations after they are made.
